What We Do

At HopeOnWheels, it is our mission to provide the homeless with the resources they need to find employment. According to the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals, poverty is one of the most pressing issues in the world today, and homelessness is a huge factor. That it is why it is critical that we help the homeless find stable and supportive jobs, as it is one of the best ways to support them. Our company aims to support the homeless population by creating mobile job centers that will have the resources they need to find employment, including computers for job searches, resume building services, and even interview preparation services.
